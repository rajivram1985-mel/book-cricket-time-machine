Privacy Policy — Book Cricket Time Machine

Last updated: 9 July 2026

Book Cricket Time Machine is a free, nostalgic browser game. The short version: we collect nothing, track nothing, and have no servers that could hold your data even if we wanted them to.

What data the app collects

None. The app has no user accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. Nothing you do in the game is transmitted to us or to anyone else.

What's stored on your device

Your scorebook — matches played, win/loss record, daily-challenge streaks, personal bests and sound preferences — is saved in your browser's local storage, on your device only. It never leaves your device. You can wipe it at any time with the “Reset data” link in the app's footer.

Backups

The “Back up” button saves your scorebook as a file to your own device's downloads. That file goes wherever you choose to put it — it is never uploaded anywhere by the app.

Network use

The app downloads its own game files (pages, images, commentary audio clips) from our web host when you first load or update it, like any website. These requests carry no personal information beyond what any standard web request contains. After the first visit, the game also works offline.

Children

The game is safe for all ages and, since it collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children either.

Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices ever change (for example, if optional online features are added someday), this page will be updated before those changes ship, and the app's store listing will reflect it.
